VOREX Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how VOREX (the “App”), provided by Jeremy Rodriguez, an independent developer operating under the name Verntro Labs (“we,” “us,” or “our”), handles your information. For data-protection purposes, the data controller is Jeremy Rodriguez. VOREX is a strength-training tracker for iPhone and Apple Watch. We designed VOREX to keep your data in your own hands: it is stored on your device and in your private Apple iCloud, and we do not operate our own servers or user accounts.

1. Information in the App

VOREX does not require you to create an account or give us your name, email, or phone number. The information in the App consists of:

  • Profile you set: a display name and an optional profile photo you choose.
  • Training data you log: workouts, sets, weights, reps, routines, personal records, achievements, and the bodyweight you enter.
  • Health data (with your permission): see Section 3.
  • App preferences: units, rest-timer and notification settings.

2. Where your data is stored

Your training data and profile are stored on your device and synced through your private iCloud (Apple’s CloudKit private database), tied to your Apple ID, so it stays available across your own Apple devices. This is your personal iCloud space — we cannot see or access it. We do not run our own backend, and we do not copy your data to servers we control. Apple’s handling of iCloud data is governed by Apple’s Privacy Policy.

3. Apple Health (HealthKit)

If you grant permission, VOREX interacts with Apple Health as follows:

  • Reads (on-device only): heart-rate variability, resting heart rate, and sleep, used solely to calculate a “recovery readiness” indicator. This processing happens on your device. This health data is never stored in iCloud and is never sent to us or any third party.
  • Writes (one-way): your completed workouts, and the bodyweight you enter in the App, can be saved to Apple Health so they appear in the Health app. VOREX does not read your bodyweight back from Apple Health.

We do not use Health data for advertising, marketing, or data-mining, and we do not disclose it to third parties. You can change Health permissions any time in iOS Settings › Privacy & Security › Health.

4. Optional social features

These features are off by default and only share data if you turn them on:

  • Community leaderboard: if you opt in, your display name, your estimated one-rep-max for board lifts, and — for the bodyweight-relative board — your bodyweight are published to a leaderboard visible to other VOREX users. This is shared through Apple’s CloudKit public database.
  • Friends: if you opt in by adding a friend via a friend code, your display name and your weekly training volume are shared with the friends you connect with.

You can leave these features at any time from within the App. Leaving stops further sharing and removes the entries you have published. The bodyweight used here is the value you enter in VOREX, not data read from Apple Health.

5. Subscriptions and payments

VOREX Pro is an auto-renewable subscription sold and processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment card or billing details. Apple may provide us with aggregated, non-identifying sales and subscription information. Manage or cancel your subscription in iOS Settings › your name › Subscriptions.

6. Third parties, advertising, and tracking

VOREX contains no third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs. We do not track you across apps or websites, we do not sell or “share” your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising, and we do not profile you for ads. The only external services involved are Apple’s (iCloud/CloudKit, HealthKit, and App Store payments), used to provide the App.

7. Notifications

If you enable them, VOREX uses local notifications for rest-timer alerts and training reminders. These are generated on your device; you can disable them in iOS Settings › Notifications.

8. Data retention and deletion

Your data remains until you delete it. You can export your full training history as a CSV file, and you can permanently erase everything using Reset All Data in the App’s Settings. Reset All Data deletes your data from the device and from your iCloud, and — if you used the social features — also removes the records you published to the leaderboard and Friends boards.

9. Your rights

Because your data lives in your own iCloud and on your device, you control it directly. Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, port, or restrict processing of your personal data:

  • EEA/UK (GDPR): rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection. Our legal basis for processing is performance of the service you request and your consent (for Health access and the optional social features).
  • California (CCPA/CPRA): rights to know, delete, correct, and opt out. We do not sell or share personal information, and we do not use sensitive personal information for purposes requiring an opt-out.

You can exercise access, portability, and deletion yourself in-App (CSV export and Reset All Data). For any other request or question, contact us (Section 12).

10. Children

VOREX is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age of digital consent in your country), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided information, contact us and we will help remove it.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the App evolves. Material changes will be reflected here with an updated “Last updated” date. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance.
